Welcome to my website

I'm thrilled to have you here! My name is Ojaswee, and I am a student in the BS-MS Dual Degree program in Data Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Bhopal (IISER Bhopal). I am currently working on my MS thesis at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur (IIT Jodhpur) under the supervision of Dr. Mayank Vatsa and Dr. Richa Singh. My work here revolves around crafting robust adversarial patches for satellite imagery. Previously, I have worked on the Robustness Benchmarking of Image Classifiers for Physical Adversarial Attack Detection as part of my BS thesis under the supervision of Dr. Akshay Agarwal. Currently, my research interests lie around crafting adversarial attacks for real-world applications.

I have also studied Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Time Series Analysis and Forecasting, Spatial Data Science, Algorithms, etc., as part of the BS-MS program at IISER Bhopal. I also have a keen interest in entrepreneurship and startup case studies. My life goal is to use my skills to make technology more accessible to the masses and thus make the world a better place.
